How to use

  • Choose **N3** or **N2** in the level dropdown.
  • Look at the kanji, try to recall readings and meaning, then press **Reveal**.
  • Use **I know this** / **Don't know yet** to track your progress.
  • Press the **speaker icons** to hear the kanji or example sentence with device TTS.

Built as a static app for GitHub Pages. TTS availability depends on your browser and OS.
